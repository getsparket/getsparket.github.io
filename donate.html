Sparket.
Turn Old Electronics into Donations

Text Sparket with details about your electronics. Our algorithms create a cash donation value. You pick a local non-profit. We send a driver to you to collect. All in 20 minutes or less.
Try It

Three Easy steps

1
Text Us
Give us your number or text us at +1 415-906-4777. Tell us what device you have and where you want the donation to go. We will donate to any Pittsburgh based 501(c)(3).
2
Get an Offer in Seconds
Our algorithms develop a cash value for your device in seconds.
3
A Car Comes to You to Collect
We have drivers who will come to your door, and then give cash to the nonprofit of your choice. A tax form is sent to you for tax purposes within one week.
